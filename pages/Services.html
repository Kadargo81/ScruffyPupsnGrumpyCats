Dog Walking

Dogs benefit from regular walks. Dog walks provide environmental enrichment, exercise, and an opportunity for social interaction as well as relieving the boredom in their day. Dogs that are not provided enough environmental enrichment can develop behavioural issues and may become destructive, depressed or fearful.

Our Dog Walking Service provides the perfect solution for busy pet guardians with pressing work or family commitments or injured or disabled pet guardians, who aren’t able to walk their pets.

ScruffyPups n’ GrumpyCats Pet Services can provide your dog with a 30 minute or 45 minute walk. Regular dog walking during the week can be provided. We also recommend our Dog Walking service when booking our Pet Sitting services.

Our priority is the safety and well-being of your dog. We will only walk dogs from the same ‘family’ together.

ScruffyPups n’ GrumpyCats Pet Services endeavour to make your dog’s walk as stimulating and enjoyable as possible.

All dog walks include the following:

  • Security – All dogs remain on a leash at all times. We use a long leash at the beach or the park to allow your dog more freedom, whilst ensuring their security. Your dog will never be left unattended and whilst in our care, our focus will be %100 on their safety and well-being.
  • Safety – In the interest of your dog’s safety, we will not walk your dog during extreme weather conditions; either extreme heat or thunderstorms. In lieu of walking your dog, we may in consultation with you provide in-home play time, environmental enrichment games and companionship.
  • Basic Training – As an experienced dog trainer I am passionate about Pet Dog Training. I can provide some basic training for your dog using ‘Force Free’ methods or ensure that any ‘Force Free’ training you have in place with your dog is practiced and maintained. (I do not and will not use any aversive training methods.)
  • Comfort – To ensure your dog is left comfortable after his or her walk, we will check your dog for prickles and grass seeds and remove them or any other debris from their fur and give them a quick brush as necessary. If your dog is wet (from a romp at the beach, or from dew or rain) we will towel dry them.
  • Equipment – ScruffyPups n’ GrumpyCats Pet Services provides water bowls, long leashes, poo bags and treats for our dog walks. We also carry with us at all times a Pet First Aid Kit, spare leashes, collars and harnesses, brushes, sunscreen and towels as well as our mobile phone with emergency contact details.

You can pay for 9 Dog Walk services at a time and receive the 10th one free.

Pet Sitting Visits

Being away from your pet or needing assistance to care for your pet can cause your pet to be stressed. This stress can be further exacerbated when they are away from their familiar environment. Our Pet Sitting Visits allow your pet to stay within the comfort of their home environment, with the familiar scents and sounds. Let us give you peace of mind while you are away or incapacitated by allowing us to care for your Pet in their own home. During our Complimentary Consultation, we will meet both you and your pet, understand the layout of your home and location of your pet’s supplies, complete a security check of the yard, and together work out the best Service to meet both yours and your pet’s needs. We can provide up to 3 Pet Sitting Visits a day. Each visit is for 60 minutes.


Our Pet Sitting Visits are a perfect solution for:

  • Working pet guardians who need someone to just ‘pop in’ and check on their beloved pets, give them a ‘potty break’, refresh their water, administer regular medication and provide companionship.
  • Pet guardians with young pups adjusting to life within a human family, or new adopted pet family members, settling in to a new environment or elderly pets all of which may be needing extra care, cuddles and ‘potty breaks’.
  • Devoted pet guardians who are away from home for holidays, work conferences, or family emergencies, and understand that their pets feel more comfortable and relaxed in their own familiar environment.
  • For injured or disabled pet guardians who need assistance caring for their beloved pets in their own home environment.

Our Pet Sitting Visits include:

  • Feeding and replenishing water bowls.
  • Poop scoop and or litter change.
  • Cleaning of any bedding and or enclosures.
  • Daily monitoring and recording of your pet’s health (general appearance, mood, food & water intake, urination and bowel movements etc.)
  • Regular updates provided during the Service Period.
  • Environmental Enrichment games, free play, and supervised exercise in secure, enclosed yard.
  • Basic grooming – removal of any debris from fur and brushing.
  • Egg collection (for chooks etc.)
  • Mail collection.
  • Rubbish bin collection.
  • Plant watering.
  • Lights on/off, curtains opened/closed.

A Dog Walk can be added to this service at a discounted price. Please enquire when making your booking.

Pet Sitting - Overnight Stays

Our Overnight Stay Service provides premium pet sitting care in your home for your pet overnight from 5pm to 9am the next morning. We will arrive at your house at the same time each afternoon and leave at the same time each morning.


This service is perfect for:

  • Pets whose guardian’s normal routine is to leave the house in the morning returning home in the evening.
  • Pet’s that have difficulty adapting to a new routine or guardians who prefer that their pet’s routine is as unchanged as possible during their absence may prefer this service.
  • Elderly or pets or pets with medical or physical conditions who require more monitoring and would benefit from the attention and care an Overnight Service can provide.

This Overnight Stay Service includes a Dog Walk, however, an additional Dog Walk can be booked at a discounted price. Please enquire when making your booking.

Pet Transport

During the care of our client’s pets, it may sometimes be necessary for us to transport pets, to the park or beach or to seek treatment.


Our Pet Transport Service is also a perfect solution for:

  • Pet guardians who need their pet taken to the Vet, the groomers, or Doggy Day Care while they are at work or busy with other commitments.
  • Pet guardians who don’t have access to a car or who don’t have a suitable car, or perhaps don’t have a crate.
  • For injured or incapacitated pet guardians who need assistance transporting their pets to appointments.

Our Pet Transport Vehicle is a fully insured, registered and maintained, modern, climate controlled SUV. We carry the following equipment in the vehicle at all times:

  • Crates and seatbelt harnesses for dogs
  • Pet First Aid Kit
  • Spare collars and leashes
  • Water and water bowls
  • Poo bags
  • Toys
  • Treats
  • Blankets
  • Towels & pet wipes

This service is by appointment only, unless it is provided as part of another service. When provided separately to our other services it must be booked 7 days in advance.
