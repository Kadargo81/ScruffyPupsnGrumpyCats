About Me

Hi, I'm Colleen.

Having worked for a large multination corporation for many years I decided it was time to follow my passion working with animals. I have been a volunteer dog trainer for nearly a decade, working with local community organisations to provide affordable training options for pet owners, while also providing education for community members and schools. I continue to volunteer my time to these organisations in the interest of improving the lives of pets and pet owners living in my local community.

I have a Cert IV in Training and Assessment & Cert II in Animal Care. I am a certified Pet Dog Ambassador Trainer, a member of The Pet Professional Guild and currently completing a Diploma in Canine Behaviour.
